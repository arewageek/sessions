# Sessions

## Overview

Sessions is a digital platform where creators can share videos and engage with their audience. The system handles video uploads, fan interactions, and monetization through a secure blockchain-based contract.

## Core Features

### 1. Video Management

Creators can:

- **Upload videos** with custom minting limits and prices
- **Adjust minting limits** for their videos
- **Update pricing** for video access

### 2. Social Interactions

Users can:

- **Like/unlike** videos
- **Post comments** on videos
- **Follow/unfollow** creators
- **View engagement metrics** (likes, comments, followers)

### 3. Monetization

- **Mint videos** (purchase access)
- **Tip creators** directly
- **Revenue sharing** between creators, platform, and minters

### 4. Creator Profiles

- **Customizable profiles** with metadata
- **Track performance metrics** (videos, followers, tips)
- **Manage content settings**

## For Creators

### Getting Started

1. **Upload your first video** by specifying:

   - A unique media ID
   - How many copies can be minted
   - The mint price

2. **Set up your profile** with descriptive information

3. **Engage with fans** through comments and likes

### Earning Money

- Receive payments when fans:
  - Mint your videos
  - Send you tips
- Automatic revenue splits (60% creator / 30% platform / 10% minter by default)

## For Users

### Discovering Content

- Browse videos and creator profiles
- See how many mints/likes/comments each video has
- View creator follower counts

### Supporting Creators

- Mint videos to access exclusive content
- Send tips to your favorite creators
- Like and comment to show appreciation

## For Administrators

### Platform Management

- Configure revenue sharing percentages
- Set platform fees
- Update admin wallets
- Monitor contract balance
- Adjust global limits (max prices, mint limits)

## Important Notices

- All transactions are permanent and recorded on the blockchain
- Video minting requires payment in cryptocurrency
- Creators control their content's availability and pricing
- The platform takes a percentage of all transactions
